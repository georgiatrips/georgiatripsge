Our Story

Who We Are

Georgia Trips was founded in 2017 by a group of local travel enthusiasts who wanted to share the beauty of Georgia with the world. Starting from Batumi, we have grown into a full-service travel company covering tours across the entire country and beyond.

We specialize in private and group tours, comfortable vehicle rentals with professional drivers, and fully customized travel packages — all designed around one goal: making your trip genuinely memorable.

From the ancient monasteries of Mtskheta to the snow-capped peaks of Kazbegi and the Black Sea shores of Adjara, we know every road, every hidden gem, and every local secret worth sharing.
