Who We Are

Passionate Locals,
Expert Guides

Georgia Trips was founded in 2017 by a group of passionate Georgian travellers and hospitality professionals who believed their country deserved to be seen, heard and experienced beyond the tourist checklist.

Today, we are a team of 20+ certified guides, drivers and travel planners based in Tbilisi, operating tours across every corner of this extraordinary country — from the towers of Svaneti to the shores of Batumi.

We take pride in small groups, personal attention, authentic local experiences and making every traveller feel like they are exploring Georgia with a friend, not a stranger.
